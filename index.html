Ornitweelogy

What is this?

Ornitweelogy is a silly little audio experiment by @quickredfox that attempts to convert tweet contents into audible chirps similar to birds. It's basically digital birds reading you tweets about birds.

All sounds are generated with javascript using Pedro Ladaria's "riffwave.js".

The background image belongs to Tim Profitt-White.

Pseudo Warning

Before you launch the experiment, I suggest you make sure you have your headphones on and the volume isn't too loud.

To launch the experiment, simply click on the "Launch" button bellow. It may take a few seconds before it starts.
